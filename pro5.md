## Claim Guardrails

Do not claim or imply experience with the following unless supported by new project evidence:

- Apache Kafka, message streaming, or Kafka-based event-driven architecture
- High-throughput systems or specific request-volume claims
- Low-latency systems or latency optimization
- Elasticsearch, OpenSearch, vector databases, or semantic search
- Java, Quarkus, or native-image frameworks
- DynamoDB or feature-store serving
- Embedding or machine-learning inference services
- AWS Bedrock
- Vietnamese NLP or multilingual search
- Ownership of CI/CD or Kubernetes platform architecture
- Performance improvements, percentages, volumes, or adoption numbers that were not measured

Prefer `worked with`, `used`, or `supported services deployed through` when describing infrastructure operated by another team. Use `designed`, `built`, `implemented`, or `owned` only for work personally completed.
